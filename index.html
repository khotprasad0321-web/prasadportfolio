How I work
actually

empathize

I start by understanding user needs, business goals, and market challenges through research, surveys, and user interviews. This helps me uncover real problems and emotional pain points that guide my design direction.

define

I translate research insights into clear design goals. Defining the core problem and success metrics ensures that every design decision aligns with the product vision and user expectations.

ideate

I explore creative possibilities through brainstorming, sketching, and wireframing. My focus is on generating innovative yet practical design solutions that balance aesthetics with usability.

prototype

I convert concepts into interactive prototypes to visualize user flows and experiences. This stage helps validate design ideas and refine interactions before moving into final development.
